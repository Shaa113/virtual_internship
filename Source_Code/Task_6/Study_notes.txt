 Study Notes
Definitions:
Feature Map: The output generated after a convolution kernel scans the input
.
Optimizer: The algorithm (like Adam or SGD) that adjusts the network's weights to minimize error
.
Advantages: Automatic feature extraction (no manual work needed) and translation invariance (recognising a cat anywhere in the frame)
.
Limitations: High computational cost and poor generalization to small image transformations like rotation
.
Future Scope: NAS (Network Architecture Search), which uses AI to design even better AI models
.
